# Engineering Glossary

Version: 2.0

---

# Purpose

Provide consistent terminology across documentation, code, and AI interactions.

---

## AI Gateway

Runtime responsible for routing AI requests.

---

## Provider

External AI or third-party service.

---

## Runtime

Execution environment coordinating requests.

---

## Feature

A user-facing capability.

---

## Module

A cohesive engineering unit.

---

## Service

Business logic layer.

---

## Repository

Persistence layer.

---

## Workflow

Ordered engineering process.

---

## Job

Asynchronous task.

---

## Pipeline

Multi-stage processing flow.

---

## Prompt

Structured instruction for an AI model.

---

## Credit

Internal unit used to measure AI generation consumption.

---

## Wallet

Internal balance owned by a user.

---

## Generation

The process of producing AI output.

---

## Circuit Breaker

Mechanism that temporarily stops requests to failing services.

---

## Fallback

Alternative provider or strategy used when the primary option fails.

---

## Technical Debt

Known engineering compromise scheduled for future improvement.

---

## Definition of Ready (DoR)

Minimum conditions required before implementation begins.

---

## Definition of Done (DoD)

Minimum conditions required before a feature is considered complete.
